documentary holds your thoughts and ideas.

documentary is a repository of provable public knowledge. documentary uses blockchain technology to assure authenticity of information. With documentary you can prove ownership and time of publication of all your ideas, knowledge and information. Use documentary to publish what matters for you. Use documentary to search and explore the ideas of others and our common knowledge. Thanks to using blockchain technology, documentary is trustworthy, unstoppable and accessible by everyone:




Trustworthy.

All your publications are stored on the Ethereum blockchain with a time stamp provided by the blockchain. Neither the content nor the time of your publications can be changed or manipulated in any way.

Publish »

Unstoppable.

The Ethereum blockchain is fully distributed. The data you put on the block chain via documentary cannot be deleted. The Ethereum blockchain cannot be stopped by any entity or authority.

Search and Explore »

Accessible by everyone.

Your publications can be read without incurring transaction costs, making it available for everyone. All code and information required to access the data on documentary is publicly available.
